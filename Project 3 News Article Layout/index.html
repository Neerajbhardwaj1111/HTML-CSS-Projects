India Secures Historic T20 Series Win Against Australia

By Priya Menon | August 17, 2025 · New Delhi

India clinched a thrilling victory over Australia in the final T20 match at Eden Gardens on Saturday, sealing the three-match series 2–1.

The final came down to the last over, with India’s all-rounder Rohan Varma hitting a decisive six to guide his team to victory.

Fans across the country celebrated late into the night, praising the team’s fighting spirit. Analysts noted that India’s bowling lineup, especially young pacer Arjun Singh, played a crucial role in turning the match around.

Sports experts are already calling this one of the most memorable T20 series in recent years.

"This is not just a win, it’s a statement of resilience," — Captain Rahul Verma
